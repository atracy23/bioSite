Craig Tracy's Hobbies

Home Remodeling

In his spare time, Craig loves working on the big old farmhouse that he and his wife bought to house all their kids. He is currently working on building an office for his wife that is about to start her new career in software development. He gutted an indoor patio attached to the garage. He insulated, drywalled, mudded, and painted the walls. He installed new windows, siding, and shingles. He leveled the floor and installed new trim. He also added several more outlets.

Some of Craig's more recent projects include completely gutting and rebuilding a second floor bathroom for his three daughters. He replaced the flooring with a newly leveled, tile floor. He put in a new vanity, sink, and mirror. He replaced the tub/shower combination and toilet. He put in new lights and exhaust fan. With the help of his daughters, he repainted all the walls. He also removed all of the wallpaper in all three of the upstairs bedrooms, orange peeled the walls, and painted.

Some of his original projects included installing new lighting, a new front door, and a porch swing on the front porch. He built a batting cage for his family in the backyard as well as a practice field for volleyball. He put in new pipes throughout the house using new pex pipes and a water manifold system. He also built a bedroom in the basement for one of his sons so everyone could have their own rooms. Craig's next project is going to be rewiring and refinishing the movie room.
